What is advocacy?

Our Advocacy service was set up to help guide people through the labyrinth of processes involved in requesting statutory services from local authorities, such as social care, home adaptations and accessible housing. Too often we have to be there for people when the system simply cannot keep up with the speed of MND. We take action to help you say what you want, secure your rights, represent your interests and to obtain services you need.

We are on your side

If you need help accessing statutory services or feel your rights are being infringed in any way, we’re here to help.
